Planetary motion :

Planetary motion, the graceful ballet of celestial bodies navigating the cosmic stage, is a captivating spectacle that has fascinated astronomers for centuries.At the heart of this intricate dance lies the gravitational embrace between planets and their parent stars, a force that governs their orbits and shapes their trajectories through space. From the innermost planets, tracing swift arcs around their brilliant sun, to outer giants, meandering in stately ellipses across the vast expanse of solar system.
